# ublock-hide-autotrader-ads
A [uBlock Origin](https://github.com/gorhill/uBlock) filter list to hide pesky content on Autotrader.

# Usage
1. Copy the content of [list.txt](list.txt) to your clipboard
2. Click on to uBlock Origin icon in your browser toolbar > `Dashboard` (cogs icon) > `My Filters` tab
3. Scroll to the bottom and paste the content into the text box
4. Click `Apply changes`

Your future Autrotrader searches will hide the sponsored content.
